Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This privacy policy applies to personal automation systems operated by James Morse ("I", "me", or "my") for residential use.

What Data Is Collected

This system collects and processes the following data solely for the purpose of operating a personal home automation system:

  • Mobile phone number of the system owner
  • Vehicle GPS location data (used only for geofencing around the owner's residence)
  • Garage door status (open/closed state)
  • SMS message content sent to and from the system

How Data Is Used

All collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Send SMS notifications to the system owner regarding garage door status
  • Receive and process commands from the system owner via SMS
  • Determine proximity of the owner's vehicle to their residence

Data Sharing

No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third parties for marketing or any other purposes. Data is processed only by the owner for personal residential automation use.

Data Retention

No personal data is stored beyond what is necessary for real-time operation of the automation system. No logs of SMS messages or location data are retained.
